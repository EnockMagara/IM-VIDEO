üìñ ABOUT THE JOURNEY

The Story Behind the Film

üè† The Beginning

It started with an empty instant noodles cup and a student in a bathrobe. A moment of hunger sparked an idea: what if we could create a gourmet meal using only what's available in a dorm?

The journey from empty cup to creative cooking begins.

üõí The Quest

From the D2 dining hall to the convenience store, watch as our protagonist gathers the most unlikely ingredients. Rice, bread, banana, butter, Pop-Tarts, chips, cookies, M&M's, jerky, soda, and smuggled ketchup packets.

ü§î The Choice

"Dorm Gourmet" captures that universal student dilemma: order expensive delivery food or try to make something creative with what you have. It's a story of resourcefulness, creativity, and the adventure of dorm life.
